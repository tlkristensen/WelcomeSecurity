# Security Policy

WelcomeSecurity takes security seriously. We appreciate the security research community
and welcome responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

## Reporting Guidelines

1. Email security reports to security@welcomesecurity.net
2. Include detailed information about the vulnerability
3. Allow reasonable time for us to respond and fix issues
4. Do not access or modify user data
5. Do not perform denial of service attacks

## Response Timeline

- We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- Initial assessment within 72 hours  
- Resolution timeline depends on severity and complexity

Thank you for helping keep WelcomeSecurity and our clients secure!
